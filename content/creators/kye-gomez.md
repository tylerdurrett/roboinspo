Kye Gomez leads the Swarms community and develops the swarms Python framework. His work focuses on hierarchical swarm structures where "Director" agents oversee specialized "Worker" agents, pursuing what he calls "agora" style research.

Known for aggressive, high-velocity development and rapid implementation of the latest research papers, Kye represents the experimental cutting edge of multi-agent systems. His GitHub repositories include curated lists of multi-agent research papers that have become essential references for the community.
