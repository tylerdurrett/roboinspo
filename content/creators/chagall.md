Chagall van den Berg represents the cutting edge of live performance integration with TouchDesigner. Her work focuses on the body as controller, utilizing Mi.Mu gloves and motion capture to drive audio-visual systems in real-time. Workshops through The NODE Institute and Music Hackspace cover the intersection of wearables, electronic music, and visual performance.
