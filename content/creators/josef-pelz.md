Josef Pelz occupies a unique space as both artist and tool-maker. He created T3D, a custom operator family that brings 3D texture functionality to TouchDesigner. His resources focus on physics simulations, reaction-diffusion systems, and extending TouchDesigner's capabilities through custom development.
