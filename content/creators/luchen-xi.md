Luchen Xi represents a fine-art approach to TouchDesigner education. Their Udemy courses provide systematic training in generative visuals, interactive control systems (Kinect, Leap Motion), and complete project workflows. Their work focuses on the intersection of TouchDesigner with traditional art concepts for the high-end digital art market.
