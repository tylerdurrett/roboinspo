Acrylicode is a duo known for making TouchDesigner accessible to newcomers. Their tutorials focus on geometric abstractions, instancing techniques, and audio-reactive visuals without overwhelming beginners with complex Python or GLSL. They also host intensive bootcamp courses for those ready to dive deeper.
