The Interactive & Immersive HQ, founded by Elburz Sorkhabi, is a leading educational platform focused on professional development in TouchDesigner. It offers both free tutorials and a PRO membership with over 200 hours of content, tools, and resources for building interactive installations and immersive experiences.
